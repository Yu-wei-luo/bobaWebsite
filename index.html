~ Bland, but not Empty ~

Read from left to right, up to down.



Paragraphs, created for your viewing.
Your full attention will be apprieciated!

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

A bunch of letters strung into words,
then strung into phrases, then sentences.

A bunch of sentences strung into paragraphs,
then strung into much more.

No one word will mean more than two,
no two words will be simpler than one.

Don't settle for only one, nor two,
go for more, more than last time, than ever.

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

Stringing text together is really fun,
I've experienced it, you should too.
